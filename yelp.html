My Work

Yelp

During the Summer of 2014, I worked as a Software Engineering intern for Yelp at their San Francisco HQ. I worked on their Internationalization team (i18n) building internal and external tools to facilitate Yelp expanding to new countries.

It was a fantastic experience - I learned about working in a large code base for the first time and getting work done on a big team. I was able to further hone my skills on SCRUM development with weekly code sprints, as well as overall task management and task estimation skills. My favorite part, though, was all of the awesome people I got to meet during my time there!
